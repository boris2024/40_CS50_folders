Johannes Vermeer Vincent Van Gogh is one of my favorite painters.
In 2018 I had the opportunity to visit Amsterdam and appreciate his works.
It is quite remarkable that he managed to create more than 2000 artworks during his short life.
He died at the early age of 37.


These are my favorite paintings:

  2. The Bedroom
  3. Vase with Irises
