Green Wheat Field with Cypress




This painting was finalized in 1889 while Vincent was a patient at the mental hospital in Saint-Rémy
While hospitalized Vincent was allowed to make excursions under supervision, he used these
opportunities to capture the beauty of various wheat fields

This painting is part of the permanent collection of the National Gallery Prague, in Prague, Czech Republic
National Gallery in Prague Staroměstské nám. 12 110 15 Prague 1 Czech Republic
